Crystal Collector

Instructions:


  1. You will be given a random number to the start of the game."Target Number"
  2. There are four crystals below.
    • Topaz
    • Emerald
    • Ruby
    • Diamond
  3. Each crystal is assigned a specific amount of points.
  4. Click a crystal to add x amount of points to your total score
  5. You win points by matching your total score to the random number.
  6. If your score goes above the random number, you lose.
  7. You won't know the value of each crystal until you click on it.
